Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic dish made with tender chicken pieces cooked with basmati rice, a blend of spices, and fresh herbs. This traditional Indian dish is known for its rich, savory taste and is often enjoyed with raita or a side of salad. The slow-cooking process allows the spices to infuse the rice and chicken, creating a mouthwatering and satisfying meal.
